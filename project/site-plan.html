Site Plan: Culinary Creativity – Fusion Recipes

Site Name

Culinary Creativity – Fusion Recipes

This name reflects the blending of Zimbabwean flavours with international cuisine, highlighting creativity and cultural pride.

Site Purpose

The site will showcase Zimbabwean fusion recipes, provide step-by-step guides, and explore food culture through blogs. It celebrates heritage while embracing global flavours.

Scenarios

  • How can I cook a Zimbabwean dish with an international twist?
  • What flavour pairings work best when blending local ingredients with global cuisine?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #8B0000 (deep red, used for headings and accents to represent rich food tones)

Secondary Color: #F5DEB3 (wheat, used for backgrounds to evoke natural ingredients)

Typography

Heading Font: 'Merriweather', serif – for a traditional, cultural feel.

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – for readability in recipes and blog posts.

Wireframe

Mobile View: Stacked layout with logo, navigation, featured recipe, and blog preview.

Desktop View: Two-column layout with navigation bar on top, featured recipe in main content, and blog sidebar.
